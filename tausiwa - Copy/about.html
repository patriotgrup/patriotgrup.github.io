About Our Company

For all the information you may need on events and festivals in Kenya, Africa and the World over, we are Tausiwa.

We cover weddings, graduation ceremonies, kids festivals, sporting events, concerts, marathons & runs, car racing rallies and many more.

Whether you're an event organizer or you want to attend some event, or check on a past popular event, we'll have you fully informed. Comprehensive reliable information should be free & at your disposal: that is what we believe.
